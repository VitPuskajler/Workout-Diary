Quick overview


Training Session:
Provide input for your pre-created workout plan. During your training session, open this tab and enter your info from the workout

Example:
Training Session for Upper Body: Bench Press - 3 sets of 10 reps, Deadlift - 3 sets of 8 reps

Workout Plan:
Information about all your exercises and mesocycles so far. Also, you can create a new mesocycle plan throughout this page

Example:
Plan includes: Week 1 - Upper/Lower split, Week 2 - Push/Pull/Legs. All placed in Mesocycle period

.

Create New Mesocycle Plan:
Create a new mesocycle plan and populate the table

Example:
New Plan: 4-week Hypertrophy-focused routine. Recommended 4-8 weeks

Username:
In this tab, you can change your exercises, passwor and more

Example:
Editing Bench Press: 2 sets of 10 reps insted 4 sets of 12 reps

What you need to know

RPE:
Rating of perceived exertion

RPE Details:
Scale 1 to 10. RPE 10 mean you could do no more rep

Sets:
How many sets you have planned to do

Sets Details:
Total number of sets for specific exercise

Reps:
How many repetitions you did

Reps Details:
Total reps for each exercise

Pauses:
How long you want to rest between sets

Pauses Details:
Recommended rest time

Notes:
Any information relevant for your workout

Notes Details:
Additional insights or observations

About

You can manage all your workouts in one place. After the end of your mesocycle, you can view statistics (currently 'Workout Plan' tab) about your progress and based on it plan a new training period. Aim of this web app is to help you periodize, structure and manage your workouts.
