Quick overview

Training Session:
Here you directly provide input for your pre created workout plan. During your training session you will simple open this tab and enter your info from workout (weights, reps...)

Workout Plan:
Informations about all your exercises and mesocycles so far. Also you can create new mesocycle plan throughout this page

Create New Mesocycle Plan:
Create new mesocycle plan and populate the table

Username:
In this tab you can change your exercise name, sets and reps. But I am not allowing any deleting so far. If There is something wrong please create a new mesocycle plan

What you need to know before usage

RPE:
Rating of perceived exertion

Sets:
How many sets you have planned to do

Reps:
How many repetitions you did

Pauses:
How long you want to rest between sets

Notes:
Any informations relevant for your workout (if something hurt, if you were hungry...)

About

You can manage all your workouts in one place. After the end of your mesocycle, you can view statistics (currently 'Workout Plan' tab) about your progress and based on it plan a new training period. Aim of this web app is to help you periodize, structure and manage your workouts.
